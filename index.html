About Me

Hello! I'm Lei Liu, a dedicated Data Analyst with a strong foundation in statistical analysis, data visualization, and database management. My journey into data began with a fascination for uncovering hidden patterns and telling compelling stories through numbers. I believe that every dataset holds valuable insights waiting to be discovered, and my goal is to transform data into strategic recommendations that drive organizations' business growth.

Over the past 2 years, I've had the opportunity to work on diverse projects across various industries, including e-commerce, digital marketing, vendor performance. My expertise lies in cleaning messy datasets, performing in-depth exploratory data analysis, building interactive dashboards, and communicating complex findings to non-technical stakeholders.

Outside of work, I enjoy hiking and reading. I'm always eager to learn new technologies and methodologies in the ever-evolving world of data. Feel free to connect with me to discuss data, technology, or anything in between!
